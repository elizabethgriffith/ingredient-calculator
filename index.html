Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Just a few ingredients and about 5 minutes, that’s all you need to make these delicious smoothies from scratch!

Ingredients:

  • g sliced bananas
  • g frozen strawberries
  • g plain greek yogurt
  • g low fat milk
  • g honey
  • g vanilla extract

Let's get to blending!

  1. Step 1: Add greek yogurt, milk, honey, and vanilla extract to the blender and blend until combined.
  2. Step 2: Slowly add bananas to the blended mixture and blend until creamy.
  3. Step 3: Add strawberries and blend until smooth.
  4. Step 4: Pour into serving glasses.

Optional: Garnish with mint leaves or cinnamon.
